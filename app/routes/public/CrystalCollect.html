Here's the Rules:


  1. A random number will be generated at the start of the game. This will be the "Number To Hit".
  2. There are four crystals below:
    • Diamond Crystal
    • Hexagon Crystal
    • Octagon Crystal
    • Square Crystal
  3. When you click on a crystal, a specific amount of points will be added to your total score.
  4. When your score matches the random number that's generated, you win!
  5. If you go over the random number that's generated, you lose!
  6. You won't know the value of each crystal until its value is added to your total score.
  7. Each time the game restarts (by losing/winning/reloading the page), the values of the individual crystals and the number to hit will change.
